About EduConnect SL

Bridging the educational gap in Sierra Leone through accessible digital resources.

Our Mission

EduConnect Sierra Leone is a non-profit initiative dedicated to providing equal access to quality educational materials for students across the country — regardless of their location, school, or economic background.

We believe every student in Sierra Leone deserves the chance to excel in their WASSCE, BECE, and university entrance exams — and that starts with access to the right resources, supportive community, and quality guidance.

Our Vision

A Sierra Leone where every student has equal opportunity to succeed academically and pursue their dreams.

Who We Serve

Senior secondary students, JSS pupils, teachers, and life-learners across all 16 districts of Sierra Leone.

How You Can Help

Volunteer as a tutor, donate materials, or partner with us to reach more students in rural communities.
